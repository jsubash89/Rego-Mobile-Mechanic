# ReGo Mobile Mechanic Glossary

Customer: person booking service for a vehicle.
Provider: mobile mechanic, independent shop, or dealership service operation that performs work.
Mobile mechanic: provider who services the vehicle at the customer's chosen location.
Independent shop: brick-and-mortar non-dealership provider.
Dealership: OEM/dealer service lane option.
Dispatcher: platform operator who monitors jobs and resolves assignment/SLA issues.
Support: operator who handles customer/provider/shop issues after or during a booking.
Service: catalog item such as oil change, diagnostic, brake service, or pre-purchase inspection.
Fulfillment: how the service is performed: mobile, shop, or dealership.
Booking: customer-facing reservation and estimate record.
Job: operational work item created from a confirmed booking.
Estimate: pre-service pricing breakdown; may contain TBD parts.
Invoice: post-service final bill.
Service report: provider-submitted work summary, photos, notes, recommendations, and warranty terms.
Service record: durable vehicle-history entry created from completed work.
SLA: operational deadline or response/arrival expectation.
